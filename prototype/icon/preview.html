The Life Grid

A 5×5 abstraction of the app's signature activity heatmap, set on a true iOS/macOS squircle in the project's exact palette. Cells rise in a "rising-tide" pattern from faint violet in the top-left to a violet→pink→amber peak in the bottom-right — today.

Master · 1024×1024

Heatmap, abstracted.

The heatmap is the only visual that defines this product. The icon lifts that one idea, reduces it to a 5×5 grid, and stages it in the same dark, aurora-lit world the dashboard lives in. No text. No monogram. Just the data shape — and a single cell that says you're here.

Design notes

01 — Why a heatmap

The 7×53 activity grid is the only visual that uniquely identifies this product. No other app in the user's dock has one. Lifting it to icon scale gives the brand something a monogram or generic glyph never could: instant recognition in the dock, the app switcher, and the favicon.

02 — Why 5×5

The full heatmap is 7×53. 5×5 is the largest square abstraction that still reads at 16px (where each cell becomes ~2.2px and the grid reads as a single coloured mass). It's icon scale, not data scale — the pattern is designed, not generated.

03 — The peak cell

The bottom-right cell uses the full violet → pink → amber gradient (the same one used for hero numbers in the dashboard) and gets a soft pink glow. It's the only cell that "breaks" the pattern — drawing the eye to today, the only cell that matters.

04 — The squircle

Built from four cubic Bezier segments with control offset 180 (out of 512). The smaller the offset, the fuller the corner — 229 is a perfect circle, 180 is the well-known iOS squircle value. Long sides, full corners, the silhouette Apple uses.

05 — Palette fidelity

Every colour maps directly to a token in src/index.css: --bg / --accent-1 / --accent-2 / --accent-3. If the brand ever shifts, the icon shifts with it.

06 — No text, no monogram

At 16px, text is illegible. At 1024px it's redundant. A single, designed mark ages better than a letterform and travels to favicon, dock, app switcher, About panel, and splash screen without modification.
